About Us

PETAL (Policy, Ethics, Tech, Accountability, and Law) is a dynamic club dedicated to exploring the intersection of technology and policy. We bring together students, professionals, and thought leaders to discuss, analyze, and shape the policies that govern our digital future.

Our mission is to foster informed dialogue about technology policy, promote ethical tech practices, and prepare the next generation of leaders who understand both the technical and regulatory landscapes.
